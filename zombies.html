Zombies

Train to Busan (2016)

Definitely one of my favorite zombie movies. It’s probably overhyped at this point but I’m going to keep hyping it up. It was made in South Korea and I think most of us know that South Korea and Japan make the best horror movies and South Korea is tearing up the zombie scene right now especially.
Like the title states the movie is about a train to Busan. The story revolves around a businessman father taking his daughter to go see her mother who he is divorced from. The film talks a lot about social issues where in Korean culture the businessmen place themselves in a higher authority and don’t care about anyone else but themselves. The movie makes me cry and legitimately scared because of the claustrophobic environment of a train filled with zombies. If you haven’t seen this film, you can watch it on Netflix right now if you’re interested.
